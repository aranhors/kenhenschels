About

About Dr. Henschel

Kenneth Allen Henschel, MD, is a neurologist listed with Baptist Health South Florida. His medical and postgraduate training includes the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine, internal medicine training at Allegheny General Hospital, neurology residency at SUNY University at Buffalo, and fellowships in neuroimmunology and multiple sclerosis at the University of Maryland Medical Center.
